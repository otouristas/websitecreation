A glossary is not only a definitions page. Used well, it is a **controlled vocabulary** that connects concepts your commercial pages already target — without stuffing exact-match anchors sitewide.

## Why glossaries help SEO

- **Clarity for crawlers:** Consistent definitions reduce ambiguity when the same phrase appears in many contexts.
- **Internal link targets:** Short, neutral anchors (“what is a canonical tag?”) can point to glossary entries instead of over-optimizing money pages.
- **Support for YMYL and E-E-A-T:** Transparent explanations and citations signal helpfulness.

## Workflow

1. Map your top 30 money queries to **entities** (not just keywords).
2. For each entity, decide: **pillar page**, **glossary entry**, or **supporting blog**.
3. From blogs and tools pages, link **up** to pillars and **sideways** to glossary where it helps the reader.
