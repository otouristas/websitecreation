Search Console is noisy if you open it without a script. This **one-hour loop** keeps decisions small and shippable.

## Minute 0–15: Segment reality

- Filter to your primary country and device.
- Identify **top gainers and losers** by clicks (not only impressions).
- Export or snapshot your top queries and landing URLs for the week.

## Minute 15–35: Cluster intent

- Group queries that should be satisfied by the **same** URL family.
- Flag **cannibalization** when two URLs split clicks for one intent.

Use [GSC query clustering](/tools/gsc-query-clustering) as the narrative hook; execution lives in the [keyword research & clustering](/platform/features/keyword-research-clustering) module.

## Minute 35–50: Pick three fixes

Choose only three: one **title/meta** test, one **content** block, one **internal link** adjustment. Anything more rarely ships.

## Minute 50–60: Validate

- Confirm tracking for target SERPs in [rank tracking](/platform/features/ranking-tracker).
- Log hypotheses so you do not confuse correlation with causation next week.
